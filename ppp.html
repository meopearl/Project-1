Taste of Asia

The colours and flavours of the East

Experience the vibrant flavours of Asia at dim t. Our contemporary Asian food blends tradition with modernity, served in stylish venues with friendly service. From handmade dim sum to slurp-tastic noodles, our skilled chefs use quality ingredients to recreate your favourite dishes from across Asia with a modern twist. Plus, we cater to specific dietary preferences with an extensive selection of gluten-free and vegan options, ensuring everyone can enjoy our tasty dishes.

Can’t dine in? Order dim t for takeaway and savour the flavours of Asia in the comfort of your home.
